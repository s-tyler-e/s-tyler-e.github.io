FAQ

“Wait a minute,” I hear you ask. Okay, not really. That would be creepy. “Wait a minute,” I hypothetically and/or metaphorically hear you ask:

“Can’t I just put an activity into a map app on my phone?”

I mean, of course, you could do that. You could also eat a stick. But why on earth would you ever do that? You wouldn’t, assuming you’re not of the Castoridae variety.

In all seriousness, the point of this website is to eliminate the “middleman” that is having to come up with an activity to do in the first place.

It’s a bit like rolling the dice. Why not take a chance?

"Well, what about my privacy?"

This is a very safe site. Trust me.

Okay, fine, it’s deeper than that. For two reasons:

1) The information you provide is generalized.

The site asks for an age range (never an exact age), a zip code (general vicinity),

and what time you want to do things (not exactly the most classified information. Unless, of course, you’re a secret agent. If that’s the case, you should probably get your assignments elsewhere!).

2) The website only stores your information until you close it!

There you have it. Nothing to worry about!
