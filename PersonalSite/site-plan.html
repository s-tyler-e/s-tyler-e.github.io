Overview

Purpose

Remember the last time you really wanted to go out and do something but had no idea where to start? In a world of technology and an increased desire to get things as fast as possible, sometimes there are too many options. Often, this can be discouraging and kills the fun before it even starts. This is where I come in. My new website is an interactive survey that any user can contribute a small amount of information about themselves. You “tell” it what you like, what you hate, how far you want to go, how many people you’re with, how much or little money you’d like to send, and other such info. Then, let the magic begin! The program processes the information you provide and gives you a list of activities and places that fit your needs! Why waste time browsing when you could be playing? Try it now!

Audience

Listen up, you young lovebirds and cliques. This site is all about YOU. Now, I'm not dumb; I know you've been in a situation like mentioned above. Not to worry! This website will save you. Why waste time awkwardly wondering what to do when you could get ready, get set, and go?
